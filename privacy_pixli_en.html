Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes the information collected through Pixli and how we use that information.

We don’t collect any personal data with Pixli. We don’t require you to provide any information when using Pixli and we don’t collect, share or use any information about you or your device.

If you submit a support request via e-mail, we may receive your email address and other information you provide related to your support request. We use these information only to provide support to you. We don’t share your email address unless we have your express consent.

Pixli doesn’t use or collect a user’s precise geographic location.
Pixli doesn’t contain any advertising banners, but they may contain information about our other apps with links to those apps.
Pixli may contain external links to our developer page on the App Store or to our social media pages.

Pixli is built using Unity® which access to some technical information about the player’s device in order to work properly. The collected technical data is sent to Unity® and we have no control over how Unity® uses this information. We strongly suggest to read the Unity® Privacy Policy for more details: https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please send us an email at info@tinkado.at
