Getting Started

  • Start with something simple. Some examples: a bouncing ball, a growing snowball, swimming fish, a blooming flower, or a flying balloon. Try to move something, for example a boat, from A to B.

  • A simple example with a step-by-step guide can be found here. You can also jumpstart your animations with the included examples in our app FlipKit.

  • To make it even easier to get started, FlipKit offers a selection of hand-drawn templates that you can trace and expand for your own animations. Simply press the plus button in the app and select a template.

  • Check out our Youtube Channel for more and explore our tutorials.

FAQ

Is FlipKit Plus a one-time purchase or a subscription?

It’s a one-time purchase. You pay for FlipKit Plus once and can use it forever. With the "FlipKit Plus" package, you gain access to all features and unlimited frames.

I’d like to purchase FlipKit Plus for my educational institution. Is there an option without in-app purchases?

  • We offer a special educational version for this purpose. It includes the same features as the regular version, including the "FlipKit Plus" package that unlocks all features and unlimited frames.

  • To activate the school version, we require the Organization ID and the name of the educational institution. If you're interested, please contact us at info@tinkado.at.

  • More information about our educational version

Where are my animations stored on the device, and how can I share them?

  • All animations you create are stored on your device in the "Files" app under the "FlipKit" folder, recognizable by its icon. FlipKit files can be shared and copied to another device.

  • If you're using iCloud, you can find the "FlipKit" folder under Files -> iCloud Drive -> FlipKit.

  • If you’re not using iCloud, the "FlipKit" folder is located directly under Files.

Troubleshooting

FlipKit is frozen, crashes, or animations won’t open. What should I do?

  • The most common cause is a lack of storage space. Please check if your device has enough free space and free up storage if necessary.

  • Check if your version of FlipKit is up to date. If not, connect your device to the internet. The app will then automatically update to the latest version.

  • If you’ve titled your animation, special characters like (“/“, “:”, …) may occasionally cause issues. Try renaming the title to see if this resolves the problem.

FlipKit crashes when importing images. What should I do?

Depending on your device and available storage, the number of images you can import may be limited. We recommend importing images in small batches instead of all at once. Also, ensure you have enough free disk space.

Couldn't resolve your issue with these tips?

Please email us at info@tinkado.at. You can also send us your FlipKit files (found in the "Files" app, as explained above) for us to take a closer look. If the file is very large, we recommend sending it via WeTransfer.
