Bring your drawings to life and create your own flipbook animation with FlipKit!
Searching for inspiration? Jumpstart your animations with the included examples or our templates. Check out our Youtube Channel for more and explore our tutorials.

Whether you are a beginner or a professional, FlipKit provides you all the tools needed to start animating. The intuitive user interface is also perfect to introduce kids to the world of moving pictures.

Puzzle game for kids ages 3 and up

This mosaic puzzle game with colorful animals is intuitive to use and promotes abstract and logical thinking. By moving and twisting the puzzle pieces the critters are assembled. Pixli is suitable for children aged 3 and up and is available as an app and as a physical game.

This consists of a felt pouch with colorful puzzle pieces and fun animal templates. The game can be expanded by custom mosaic figures, nurturing a child's creativity and imagination. The game is available in various versions. For more details, please visit our Etsy-Shop.

In our app, the game concept has been digitally implemented. The mosaic figures reside within a beautifully crafted world. When a character is completed, it comes to life, and a lovely animation creates a rewarding experience. The app is available on the App Store, Google Play Store and Amazon App Store.
