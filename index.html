Bring your drawings to life and create your own flipbook animation with FlipKit!

Puzzle game for kids ages 3 and up

This mosaic puzzle game with colorful animals is child-friendly explained and intuitive to use. By turning and moving the pieces animals are assembled. Finished figures then come to life and the child is rewarded with lovely animations.

The game promotes abstract and logical thinking and is particularly suitable for children ages 3 and up.
